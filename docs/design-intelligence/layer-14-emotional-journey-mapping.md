# Design Intelligence Layer 14: Emotional Journey Mapping

## Purpose

Systematic mapping of emotional journeys users experience, ensuring emotional beats are designed intentionally and create engaging experiences.

**This layer ensures emotional experiences are designed, not accidental.**

---

## Emotional Journey Structure

### Journey Phases

**Typical Phases**:
1. **Discovery** - Initial excitement, curiosity
2. **Learning** - Understanding, competence building
3. **Mastery** - Confidence, skill demonstration
4. **Exploration** - Experimentation, creativity
5. **Achievement** - Accomplishment, recognition
6. **Community** - Connection, sharing

**Why**: Phases create emotional arc.

---

### Emotional Beats

**Beat Types**:
- **Peak Moments** - High emotional intensity
- **Valley Moments** - Low emotional intensity (rest)
- **Rising Moments** - Increasing intensity
- **Falling Moments** - Decreasing intensity

**Why**: Beats create emotional rhythm.

---

## Emotional Journey Design

### Tension Curves

**Curve Types**:
- **Rising Tension** - Gradually increasing
- **Peak Tension** - High point
- **Release** - Tension release
- **Recovery** - Return to baseline

**Design Implications**:
- Build tension gradually
- Create peak moments
- Provide release
- Enable recovery

**Why**: Tension curves create engagement.

---

### Emotional Variety

**Principle**: Vary emotional experiences.

**Emotional Types**:
- **Joy** - Happiness, delight
- **Tension** - Challenge, uncertainty
- **Relief** - Problem solving, completion
- **Pride** - Achievement, mastery
- **Curiosity** - Discovery, exploration

**Why**: Variety prevents monotony.

---

### Emotional Pacing

**Pacing Principles**:
- **Peaks and Valleys** - Alternate intensity
- **Recovery Time** - Allow emotional recovery
- **Build-Up** - Gradually increase intensity
- **Payoff** - Reward emotional investment

**Why**: Proper pacing maintains engagement.

---

## Emotional Beat Design

### Discovery Beats

**Purpose**: Create "aha!" moments.

**Design Elements**:
- Hidden features
- Unexpected combinations
- Novel interactions
- Surprise rewards

**Why**: Discovery creates joy and excitement.

---

### Tension Beats

**Purpose**: Create challenge and engagement.

**Design Elements**:
- Risk/reward decisions
- Time pressure
- Uncertainty
- Stakes

**Why**: Tension creates focus and engagement.

---

### Mastery Beats

**Purpose**: Create satisfaction and pride.

**Design Elements**:
- Skill demonstration
- Optimization opportunities
- Recognition systems
- Achievement feedback

**Why**: Mastery creates pride and satisfaction.

---

### Reward Beats

**Purpose**: Create satisfaction and motivation.

**Design Elements**:
- Immediate rewards
- Meaningful rewards
- Varied rewards
- Surprise rewards

**Why**: Rewards create satisfaction and motivation.

---

## Emotional Recovery

### Recovery Design

**Purpose**: Enable recovery from negative emotions.

**Recovery Mechanisms**:
- **Failure Recovery** - Ways to recover from failure
- **Stress Relief** - Ways to reduce stress
- **Frustration Relief** - Ways to reduce frustration
- **Overwhelm Relief** - Ways to reduce overwhelm

**Why**: Recovery prevents abandonment.

---

### Recovery Timing

**When to Provide Recovery**:
- After failures
- After high tension
- When frustration builds
- When overwhelm occurs

**Why**: Timely recovery maintains engagement.

## How Cursor Uses This Layer

### When Designing Experiences

Cursor must:
1. **Map emotional journey** - Phases, beats, curves
2. **Design emotional beats** - Discovery, tension, mastery, reward
3. **Create emotional variety** - Different emotions, pacing
4. **Enable recovery** - From failures, stress, frustration

**Never design experiences without emotional journey mapping.**

---

### When Evaluating Designs

Cursor must ask:
- Is emotional journey mapped?
- Are emotional beats present?
- Is there emotional variety?
- Is recovery enabled?

**Never accept designs without emotional journey.**

---

## Success Indicators

Emotional journey mapping succeeds when:
- ✅ Emotional journey is mapped
- ✅ Emotional beats are present
- ✅ Emotional variety exists
- ✅ Recovery is enabled
- ✅ Users experience intended emotions
