# Design Audit Protocol

## Purpose

Systematic protocol for auditing design quality, ensuring designs meet standards and serve user needs.

**Design audits prevent design drift and ensure quality.**

---

## Audit Scope

### What to Audit

**Design Elements**:
- Experience pillars alignment
- Player psychology respect
- Core loop effectiveness
- Systems map coherence
- Mechanic evolution appropriateness
- UX decision consistency

**Why**: Comprehensive audit ensures design quality.

---

## Audit Process

### Step 1: Preparation

**What to Do**:
- Gather design documentation
- Review design intelligence layers
- Prepare audit checklist
- Set audit scope

**Output**: Audit preparation complete

---

### Step 2: Experience Audit

**What to Check**:
- Does design serve core fantasy?
- Are emotional beats present?
- Does it avoid "must never become"?
- Are reference inspirations applied?

**Output**: Experience audit results

---

### Step 3: Psychology Audit

**What to Check**:
- Are motivation loops served?
- Is cognitive load managed?
- Is reward timing appropriate?
- Are failure states handled?

**Output**: Psychology audit results

---

### Step 4: Loop Audit

**What to Check**:
- Is core loop clear?
- Does loop reinforce experience?
- Is feedback immediate?
- Does loop create progress?

**Output**: Loop audit results

---

### Step 5: Systems Audit

**What to Check**:
- Are systems coherent?
- Do dependencies make sense?
- Are failure modes handled?
- Do systems scale?

**Output**: Systems audit results

---

### Step 6: Synthesis

**What to Do**:
- Synthesize audit findings
- Identify issues
- Prioritize problems
- Recommend fixes

**Output**: Audit report with recommendations

---

## Audit Checklist

### Experience Quality

- [ ] Serves core fantasy
- [ ] Creates emotional beats
- [ ] Avoids anti-patterns
- [ ] Applies inspirations

### Psychology Quality

- [ ] Serves motivation loops
- [ ] Manages cognitive load
- [ ] Times rewards appropriately
- [ ] Handles failures gracefully

### Loop Quality

- [ ] Loop is clear
- [ ] Loop reinforces experience
- [ ] Feedback is immediate
- [ ] Progress is meaningful

### Systems Quality

- [ ] Systems are coherent
- [ ] Dependencies are clear
- [ ] Failures are handled
- [ ] Scaling is possible
