# Evolution Audit Checklist

## Purpose

Checklist for auditing evolution systems, ensuring experiences evolve to maintain engagement.

**Evolution audits ensure experiences stay engaging.**

---

## Audit Checklist

### Core Loop Evolution
- [ ] Loop evolution is planned
- [ ] Evolution triggers are clear
- [ ] Evolution hooks are present
- [ ] Evolution maintains engagement

### Mechanic Evolution
- [ ] Mechanics evolve appropriately
- [ ] Evolution timing is right
- [ ] Complexity increases gradually
- [ ] Depth increases appropriately

### System Evolution
- [ ] Systems evolve to support growth
- [ ] Evolution maintains balance
- [ ] Scaling is possible
- [ ] Performance is maintained

### Content Evolution
- [ ] Content evolves over time
- [ ] New content maintains quality
- [ ] Content variety increases
- [ ] Content pacing is maintained

---

**This checklist ensures evolution through systematic auditing.**
