# Pacing Audit Checklist

## Purpose

Checklist for auditing pacing, ensuring experiences maintain engagement through proper pacing.

**Pacing audits ensure engagement through proper rhythm.**

---

## Audit Checklist

### Temporal Pacing
- [ ] Session length is appropriate
- [ ] Activity duration is varied
- [ ] Wait times are reasonable
- [ ] Cooldowns are meaningful

### Mechanical Pacing
- [ ] Mechanics introduced gradually
- [ ] Complexity increases appropriately
- [ ] Unlocks are meaningful
- [ ] Features available at right time

### Emotional Pacing
- [ ] Emotional intensity varies
- [ ] Tension curves are well-designed
- [ ] Rewards are frequent enough
- [ ] Recovery periods exist

### Progression Pacing
- [ ] Progress rate is satisfying
- [ ] Milestones are meaningful
- [ ] Achievement pacing is appropriate
- [ ] Mastery progression is balanced

---

**This checklist ensures proper pacing through systematic auditing.**
