# Emotional Design Research

## Purpose

Research on emotional design principles, grounding UX decisions in emotional psychology.

**This research grounds UX in emotional psychology.**

---

## Key Emotional Design Principles

### Visceral Level

**Principle**: Immediate emotional response to appearance.

**Research Findings**:
- First impressions form quickly
- Visual aesthetics matter
- Color affects emotion
- Typography creates feeling

**Design Implications**:
- Design for first impressions
- Use aesthetics intentionally
- Choose colors carefully
- Select typography thoughtfully

**Why**: Visceral responses are immediate and powerful.

---

### Behavioral Level

**Principle**: Emotional response to functionality and use.

**Research Findings**:
- Usability creates satisfaction
- Responsiveness feels good
- Flow creates positive emotions
- Mastery feels rewarding

**Design Implications**:
- Ensure usability
- Make interactions responsive
- Create flow states
- Enable mastery

**Why**: Behavioral responses create ongoing engagement.

---

### Reflective Level

**Principle**: Long-term emotional connection and meaning.

**Research Findings**:
- Meaning creates connection
- Identity matters
- Memories drive return
- Values create loyalty

**Design Implications**:
- Create meaning
- Enable identity expression
- Create memorable moments
- Align with values

**Why**: Reflective responses create long-term engagement.
