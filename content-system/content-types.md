# Content Types

**Authority Tier**: 2 (Mandatory Process)
**Purpose**: Taxonomy of all synthesizable content.

## 1. Visual Content
*   **Sprite**: 2D raster images (characters, items).
*   **Texture**: Repeating patterns or surfaces.
*   **VFX**: Particle system configurations.
*   **UI Element**: HUD, menus, icons.

## 2. Geometric Content
*   **Tilemap**: Grid-based world layout.
*   **Level**: Topology, entity placement, navigation mesh.
*   **Shape**: Vector collision bodies.

## 3. Narrative Content
*   **Lore**: Histories, descriptions, flavor text.
*   **Dialogue**: Conversation trees.
*   **Name**: Entity names (People, Places, Items).
*   **Quest**: Objectives and rewards.

## 4. Audio Content
*   **SFX**: Sound effects (synthesized or layered).
*   **Music**: Composition structure (leitmotifs, progressions).

## 5. Mechanics Content
*   **Stat Block**: Numerical attributes (HP, STR, Speed).
*   **Ability**: Action definitions and effects.
*   **Loot Table**: Probability distributions.
