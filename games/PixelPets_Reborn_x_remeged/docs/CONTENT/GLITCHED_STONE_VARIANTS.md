# Glitched Stone Variants & Enhanced Mutation Effects

## Overview
This document details the design of glitched stone variants in Pixel Pets Reborn X Remerged. Glitched stones are special variants of existing stones that have been corrupted by chaos energy, creating enhanced mutation effects during fusion and unique properties that increase the probability and power of glitched fusion outcomes.

## Glitched Stone Formation & Origin

### Chaos Corruption Process
Glitched stones form when standard stones are exposed to concentrated chaos energy from The Grid's corrupted sectors. This exposure corrupts the stone's data structure, causing it to exhibit unpredictable properties while amplifying its base effects.

### Location of Formation
- **Chaos Zones**: Areas of The Grid where reality is unstable
- **Corrupted Sectors**: Data structures that have been damaged by errors
- **Glitch Wells**: Pools of concentrated chaotic data
- **Anomaly Fields**: Regions where normal rules don't apply

### Rarity of Glitched Stones
- **Glitched Common Stones**: 5% chance of standard stones becoming glitched
- **Glitched Rare Stones**: 8% chance of rare stones becoming glitched
- **Glitched Epic Stones**: 12% chance of epic stones becoming glitched
- **Glitched Legendary Stones**: 15% chance of legendary stones becoming glitched
- **Glitched Mythic Stones**: 20% chance of mythic stones becoming glitched

## Core Glitched Stone Properties

### Universal Glitched Properties
All glitched stones share the following base properties:
- **Mutation Amplification**: Increases mutation chance by 50%
- **Chaos Resonance**: Doubles the base 3-7% glitch chance (6-14%)
- **Data Corruption**: Alters pet's sprite DNA, causing visual mutations
- **Reality Distortion**: Minor chance to affect battle rules
- **Unpredictable Effects**: Standard stone effects become variable
- **Glitch Signature**: Marks fused pet as having enhanced glitch potential

### Visual Characteristics
- **Standard Glitch Effects**: Pixelated artifacts, color shifting
- **Intensity Level**: Varies based on stone tier
- **Color Pulsation**: Colors shift and pulse erratically
- **Data Stream**: Visual "glitch" effects flowing across surface
- **Unstable Energy**: Constantly shifting energy patterns

## Special Glitched Stone Combinations

### Glitched Stone Pairs
When two glitched stones are used in fusion, special effects occur:

#### Same-Type Glitched Pairs
- **Effect**: Doubles the base glitch chance and mutation effects
- **Example**: Glitched Ruby + Glitched Ruby = 20-36% glitch chance
- **Visual**: Enhanced glitch effects and stronger visual mutations
- **Abilities**: Higher chance for glitched abilities

#### Mixed Glitched Pairs
- **Effect**: Creates unique hybrid chaos effects
- **Example**: Glitched Ruby + Glitched Sapphire = Chaotic Fire-Water
- **Visual**: Combined glitch effects from both stones
- **Abilities**: Hybrid glitched abilities combining both elements

#### Glitched Chaos Pairs
- **Effect**: Maximum chaos when two glitched chaos stones are used
- **Example**: Glitched Opal + Glitched Opal = Reality Break
- **Visual**: Maximum glitch effects and visual chaos
- **Abilities**: Reality-bending glitched abilities

## Mutation Effects System

### Visual Mutations
- **Glitch Pixels**: Random pixel corruption on sprite
- **Color Shifting**: Colors change randomly during battle
- **Asymmetry**: Left/right sides become different
- **Visual Scars**: Permanent glitch scars on sprite
- **Data Stream**: Visual artifacts flowing across sprite
- **Size Variations**: Random size fluctuations

### Stat Mutations
- **Variable Stats**: Stats randomly fluctuate during battle
- **Power Spikes**: Random stat increases during battle
- **Inconsistent Growth**: Growth patterns become variable
- **Chaotic Scaling**: Stats scale unpredictably with tier
- **Random Buffs/Debuffs**: Stats change randomly during battle

### Ability Mutations
- **Variable Effects**: Same ability has different effects each use
- **Random Activation**: Abilities may activate unexpectedly
- **Hybrid Abilities**: Combines features of multiple ability types
- **Glitch Activation**: Abilities may trigger with visual glitch effects
- **Chaos Scaling**: Ability power becomes variable

### Behavioral Mutations
- **Random Actions**: Pet may take random actions
- **Unpredictable Targets**: May attack different targets than intended
- **Variable Timing**: Action timing becomes random
- **Chaos Loyalty**: May accidentally help or harm allies
- **Reality Confusion**: May lose track of battle state
